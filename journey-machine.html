Journey Machine

A person's path isn't a straight line. Any drop can be turned back toward paying — or it ends somewhere we didn't want. Green arrows = back on track. Red = a lost ending. The one goal is Paid; every red ending below has a fix, because those are the ones we're solving for.

The shift: a drop-off doesn't end the journey — our email tries to loop the person back onto the path. Some loops land them at Paid; some don't.
The job: make the green loops win more often, and for every red ending, have a real fix — even if the fix is a product change or simply leaving them alone.
