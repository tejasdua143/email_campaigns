Which email to trigger

Walk the branches: the last funnel stage they reached, then what they actually did. The path lights up as you go, and the leaf tells you, directionally, what to send and when. Stages match the signup → payment dashboard.

The funnel: Signup → Role pick → Doc init → Doc start → Outline generated → Editor loaded → Deck loaded → Changed slide → Payment start → Checkout → Payment success.
How it routes: first branch that fits wins (order is priority). Hard signals for now. Mechanical drops also raise a product flag, not just an email.
Always on top: frequency cap + opt-out + quiet hours can veto any send. Urgent (broke / payment failed) skips quiet hours. Every email carries a campaign_id so we know who came back through it.
